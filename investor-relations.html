Investment Thesis

Why First Canadian Graphite represents a compelling opportunity in the critical minerals sector

First Canadian Graphite is positioned at the intersection of North America's critical minerals strategy and the global energy transition. With proven graphite resources, strategic infrastructure, and a clear path to production, FCI offers investors exposure to one of the most essential battery materials markets with significant upside potential.

Development Roadmap

Key milestones and timeline for advancing the project toward production

Project Acquisition & Initial Exploration Completed 2023

Secured 100% ownership of the graphite project in Ontario. Completed initial geological surveys and confirmed high-grade graphite mineralization.

Property Acquisition Geological Mapping Initial Sampling
Phase 1 Drilling & Resource Estimate Completed Q2 2024

Completed initial drilling program with 5,000m of diamond drilling. Established maiden NI 43-101 compliant resource estimate.

5,000m Drilling Maiden Resource Metallurgical Testing
Phase 2 Expansion Drilling & PEA In Progress
